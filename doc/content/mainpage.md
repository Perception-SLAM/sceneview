Sceneview {#mainpage}
=========

Sceneview is a 3D visualization toolkit targeted at real time rendering for
scientific, engineering, and robotics applications.

## Requirements

The three requirements for Sceneview are:
- CMake (build system)
- Qt 5.4+
- Assimp
